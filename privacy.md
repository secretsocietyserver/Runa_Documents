# Runa Bot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

## Information Collected

Depending on which features are used, Runa may store:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Discord Channel IDs
* Discord Role IDs
* Ticket data and transcripts
* Giveaway data
* Leveling and economy data
* Reminder data

Runa does not sell your data. Frankly, nobody is offering to buy it.

## Why Data Is Stored

Data is stored to provide bot functionality, including:

* Moderation
* Ticket system
* Tracking levels and economy stuff
* Giveaways and reminders

## Data Retention

Data may be retained for as long as necessary for the bot to function or until it is deleted from the system.

## Third Parties

Runa operates through Discord and therefore relies on Discord's platform and services.

Users are also subject to Discord's privacy policies and terms.

## Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored information, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights

Server administrators may request deletion of their server's stored data where technically feasible.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Runa constitutes acceptance of any changes. Doesn't matter if you read them or not.

## Contact

Questions regarding privacy can be directed to Fawn through Discord.
